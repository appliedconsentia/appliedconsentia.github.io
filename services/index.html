Services that grow with your business

Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) offers a radically different way for modus operandi for enterprises. It gives enterprises the ability to operate as decentralized entities in the context of a business network without the need for mediation by a third party. To exploit a decentralized business network requires a different mindset and technology from that applied to a traditional centralized business model.

We will leverage our expertise in DLT to work with you along the lines of Lean Startup and Design Thinking methodologies. We use these methodologies in a vendor and a technology-neutral way to help you appreciate, design, implement and incept a DLT-based solution optimized to your needs.

We have also incubated DLT and related technologies, including strategy papers, that you can license from us to help build solutions related to identity management, data provenance, and orchestration.

Identity management

We have incubated DLT and strategies to help you exploit DLT for identity management.

Data Provenance

We have incubated DLT strategies and technologies to help you deploy a data provenance solution.

Business networks

We will help you research, incept, design and deploy DLT enabled business networks.

DLT orchestration

We have incubated technologies and, we will help you customise them to orchestrate DLT networks.
