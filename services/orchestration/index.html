DLT orchestration

A centralized business model is one where a single party manages transactions for all of its members. The orchestration of a centralized system has the benefit of numerous tools developed over many years.

A DLT based business network enables enterprises to operate independently and transacting with other parties. Unfortunately, there is a lack of tools to help small enterprises deploy and manage their own DLT nodes. It hinders the ability of smaller enterprises to exploit the self-sovereign feature of DLT to its maximum potential.
