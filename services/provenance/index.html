Data Provenance

The provenance of assets over their lifecycle is necessary to enable trust amongst multiple parties in a business network. The traditional system used to establish an assetâ€™s provenance involves a centralized party keeping track of the movement of assets. There are several challenges in this approach.

Firstly, relying on a single party to track the movement of assets presents a single point of failure.

Secondly, the movement of assets across multiple organizations and legal jurisdictions presents challenges from a regulatory perspective.

Thirdly, technical challenges of having all parties involved in the movement of assets to harmonize on the data format.

The importance of provenance

Applied Consentia believes that a decentralized DLT based approach is the optimal approach to managing asset provenance.

How can Applied Consentia contribute to a decentralized data provenance model?

We have worked with partners to conceptualize and built a DLT based provenance system. As exemplified by this proof-of-concept medical audit trail to track medical records.

By leveraging on our expertise and partnership, you will have access to:

  • Research latest development in DLT for provenance.

  • Incubated technology built to support DLT for provenance.
