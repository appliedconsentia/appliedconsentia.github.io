Applied Consentia

Applied Consentia is an innovative technology provider and research lab based in the UK specializing in exploiting Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) to solve real-world problems across multiple industries.

We collaborate with small, medium and large enterprises to research, design and build DLT and related technologies. We also help enterprises exploit opportunities to build decentralized business models the DLT affords. We offer this service through technologies and strategies incubated in-house in Applied Consentia or our extensive global network.

We are led by an experienced team of innovators, technologists, scientists and complemented by a network of industry advisers in Europe and Asia.

Technology provider

We have incubated technologies in-house to support these DLT use cases: identity management, data provenance and orchestration.

Resarch and Development

We provide expertise to help you appreciate and exploit the power of DLT.

Open Consentia

We engage the developers' community via our open-source initiative call Open Consentia to identify opportunities for and experiment with DLT.
